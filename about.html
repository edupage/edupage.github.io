Mission Statement

The mission of Averroes study centre is to erase the opportunity gap, arising from financial constraints of the youth in Uzbekistan, through innovative learning techniques that help to decrease time for absolute mastering of subjects and at the same time provide affordable education. Averroes is committed to deliver on its core values and goals through its Corporate Promise:

✔

University-like atmosphere of learning experience at affordable prices for the general population

✔

Increase in economic growth and reduction in poverty can be achieved through provision of an outstanding education platform, that is suitable even for the economically disadvantaged segment of population in the countries of operation

✔

Personal accountability for a student’s performance and setting his mindset as a global citizen, who is committed to create a positive change in the socium

Core Values


People

Averroes is not only about providing an outstanding service for its partners but also focusing on the self-development of the people within the company through constant encouragement, motivation and support in order for them to grow personally and become more and more competitive in the global labor market.


Enlightenment

People who come to us seeking specific knowledge should leave with a sense of newly acquired wisdom delivered by the environment and innovative curriculum, which will lay foundation for continuous self-development and perfection.


Results-Oriented

As providers of affordable education for masses, our will is to enable the ones who strive for a better future with the means and results they are righteous to have.


Persuasion

We believe that one cannot be taught without being shown the way of rational thinking, that consists of rhetorical, dialectical and demonstrative methods of consistent and persuasive reasoning. It is our aim to define a pathway towards self-development through hard work and nurturing the habit towards excellence that will mold one’s personality.
